SMS Payment Panel

Rockey Panel • July 2023 - August 2023

An Android app that listens for cash transaction SMS messages from providers such as:

  • VF-Cash and e& Cash in Egypt
  • Libyana and Almadar in Libya
  • Asiacell in Iraq

The app performs the following actions:

  • Validates incoming SMS messages
  • Parses essential details such as transaction ID, amount, phone number, and date
  • Initiates a USSD call to determine the phone number that received the SMS, then includes it in the sent data
  • Sends the extracted information along with the user's registration data to the backend
  • Displays the SMS messages in a user interface with status indicators (e.g., accepted, rejected, uploaded, default)
